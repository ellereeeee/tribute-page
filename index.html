Tom Hardy

English actor, screenwriter, and producer

"I have a very busy head. I have inside voices that I have learned to contain." -Tom Hardy

I admire Tom Hardy because he is open about his own flaws and insecurities and also because he's humble about his own successes. Tom Hardy dealt with drug and alcohol addiction, but recovered and worked hard for his now successful acting career.

Tom Hardy is the only child of his family. His mother was an artist and a painter and his father was a novelist and comedy writer. He studied at several drama schools in the UK. He dealt with and overcame an alcohol and cocaine addiction in his twenties. Tom is currently an ambassador for the Prince's Trust, a UK youth charity that provides mentoring support. He has two children and two dogs. Tom has described Gary Oldman as his personal hero. Here is a timeline of Tom Hardy's life:

  • 1977 - born in Hammersmith, London to Anne and Edward Hardy
  • 1998 - wins supermodel competition
  • 2001 - stars in Band of Brothers
  • 2001 - stars in Black Hawk Down
  • 2003 - quits his cocaine addiction
  • 2008 - stars in Bronson
  • 2008 - has a son, Louis Thomas, with his then girlfriend Rachael Speed
  • 2010 - stars in Inception
  • 2011 - stars in Tinker Tailor Soldier Spy
  • 2011 - stars in Warrior
  • 2012 - stars in The Dark Knight Rises
  • 2014 - stars in Mad Max: Fury Road
  • 2015 - married actress Charlotte Riley
  • 2015 - stars in The Revenant and receives his first Oscar Nomination

"I'm never lost in a character, ever. Everything is absolutely articulated and worked out and there's no room for error. The only thing there is room for is inspiration in the moment because you've done your homework up to that point."

- Tom Hardy
